Cloudiness


This plot compares the cloudiness percentage and latitudes of cities within the Northern and Southern hemisphere. The plot does not show a relationship between latitude and cloudiness. We can see that some cities in the dataset have zero percent cloudiness while the max cloudiness percentage for these cities is 100%. Also the shows that the cities are with 60 degree south pole and 80 degree north pole in latitude.
