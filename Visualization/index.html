Summary: Latitude vs. X

Weather data for over 500 cities within the Northern and Southern hemisphere was previously analyzed using Pandas and Matplotlib. Weather data was pulled from OpenWeatherMap API. The data contains weather information for more than 540 cities. The data contains the following information: City Name, Country, Date,Longitude, Latitude and weather conditions such as Cloudiness, Humidity, Max Temperature and Wind Speed.

Matplotlib was used to plot results of analysis from the weather data. The analysis was focused on the relationship between weather conditions ( Cloudiness, Humidity, Temperature and Wind Speed) in cities and their latitudes. The weather data plots captured weather conditions for 01/05/2017. This project creates a dashboard that provides visualizations of analysis from the weather data. It contains plots and insights gained from analysis.
