Max Temperature vs. Latitude


The plot shows that cities closer to the equator(0° Degree) based on the dataset are warmer than cities farther away from the equator. From the max temperature of cities in the North Pole begins to drop as based on their latitudes for the equator. For example cities within 60°N and 80°N have lower temperatures than cities within 20°N and 40°N. The max temperature shows that most cities in the South Pole have higher temperatures this time of the year - 01/05/17.
